A detailed operational playbook for using Claude as a production multiplier across a portfolio of 10 faceless social media accounts across Twitter, newsletters, YouTube, and content sites. The central insight is that the same 15-hour weekly time investment that lets one person manage one account manually can manage ten AI-assisted accounts — the leverage comes from treating Claude as a thinking partner with structured master prompts rather than a one-shot content generator. The article covers full portfolio architecture, weekly batch production system, niche selection strategy, product creation templates ($47 packs through $1997 programs), account management dashboards, team hiring thresholds, and a 3-year revenue/exit trajectory.

## Key Takeaways

- **The ratio**: Claude generates 100% of drafts, human filters 30% through quality control, human edits the selected 30%, then posts the refined output. AI handles production speed; human handles strategy and quality judgment.
- **Master prompt library**: Invest 20-30 hours upfront building 20-30 reusable prompt templates (thread, newsletter, course, email sequence, etc.) with voice/structure/specifics baked in. Generic prompts produce generic output; structured prompts with avatar, voice, and framework constraints produce voice-consistent content.
- **Portfolio diversification**: 3-4 core niches you actually understand, 2-3 accounts per niche with different angles. Ten completely separate niches is unmanageable strategically; three niches with overlapping funnel structure (Twitter → Newsletter → Product) is the intended architecture.
- **Sunday batch system**: 4-hour weekly session generates entire week's content for 10 accounts — 4 threads, 12 short posts, 2-3 newsletters, 3-4 blog posts, 2 YouTube scripts. Daily engagement and DM sales take 1.5 hours/day.
- **Product creation math**: 30 products across 10 accounts takes 150-270 hours with Claude vs. 900-1,800 hours manually. Templates use structured prompts specifying avatar, outcome, module structure, voice, and lesson format.
- **Exit strategy**: Year-2 portfolio doing $80k-$150k/month can be partially exited at 12-36x monthly revenue multiples; selling 3-5 accounts while keeping 5-7 yields $600k-$2M cash plus ongoing income.
