A practical guide from Sybil Solutions on removing personal information and unwanted content from the web. Particularly relevant for public figures, crypto/tech founders, and anyone with a visible online presence who faces risks from doxxing or targeted harassment. The guide emphasizes OSINT awareness — understanding how attackers find you is the first step to minimizing your exposure.

## Key Takeaways

- Proactive defense matters: understand what data is publicly available about you before attackers do
- OSINT techniques are the lens through which to audit your own data leakage
- The guide covers specific removal processes for data broker sites, search engine results, and social platforms
- Especially relevant in the current environment where "public figures" (including crypto/tech people) face elevated physical and digital risk
