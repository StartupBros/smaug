Bloomberg article about DoorDash's new paid Tasks program that compensates couriers for recording household chores to help train AI and robotics models.

## Key Takeaways

- DoorDash launched a "Tasks" app in select US markets
- Couriers earn money by submitting video and audio of household activities
- Data collected helps train humanoid robot models
- Represents real-world data collection for AI training
