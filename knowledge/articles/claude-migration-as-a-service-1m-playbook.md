A playbook for building a "Claude Migration as a Service" agency that capitalizes on Claude's memory import feature to migrate businesses from ChatGPT.

## Core Idea

Claude launched memory imports, meaning you can migrate anyone from ChatGPT to Claude in ~60 seconds. Most businesses have employees on personal ChatGPT accounts with no shared context, connected workflows, or company knowledge baked in. Claude's ecosystem (Projects, memory, skills, cowork, connectors, MCP integrations) positions it as a business operating system rather than a chatbot.

**Offer:** Claude Migration as a Service — pick one niche, go deep.

## The Playbook

1. **Pick a hyper-specific vertical.** Sales teams at automotive dealerships. Buy-side agents at commercial real estate brokerages. Being general is death.
2. **Record their workflows.** Use Wispr Flow to transcribe meetings and document every SOP they run on muscle memory.
3. **Turn SOPs into Claude skills.**
4. **Turn skills into agents.**
5. **Build a dashboard** so they react to their business instead of manually running it.

You're not selling "AI consulting." You're selling autonomy.

## Customer Acquisition

- Create vertical-specific content and lead magnets (e.g., "How AI is replacing the BDR role at car dealerships")
- Run a free workshop introducing them to Claude's ecosystem and why it beats ChatGPT for business
- Upsell a paid lunch-and-learn training on-site

## Path to $1M

- **Months 1–3:** Land 5 workshop clients → $25K. Convert 2 to retainers → $10K/mo.
- **Months 4–6:** 10 workshops/mo ($50K) + 8 retainer clients ($40K/mo) + 3 software clients ($30K/mo) = $120K/mo.
- **Months 7–10:** Referrals compound. 15 workshops ($75K) + 15 retainers ($75K/mo) + 8 software clients ($80K/mo) = $230K/mo.
- **Total by month 10:** $1M+ (conservative)

Claude just made switching free. Memory imports mean zero friction. Most businesses haven't heard of Claude Projects yet. The old automation agency playbook was Zapier/n8n workflows nobody maintained. The new one is building intelligence layers that make businesses run themselves.
