A practical workflow guide for Claude Cowork (Anthropic's desktop automation product), covering 10 real-world use cases with copy-paste prompts aimed at non-technical users. The emphasis is on file system access, inbox management, scheduling, and research automation — tasks where giving Claude direct desktop access eliminates manual steps that previously required human intervention throughout. The author frames these as proven patterns rather than theoretical examples.

## Key Takeaways

- Desktop Cleanse: grant Cowork folder access to conduct a deep clean of accumulated files
- Email Inbox Assistant: personal email management via Cowork with full inbox access
- Weekly Comprehensive Review: productivity review combining docs and calendar context
- Expense Tracker: scan desktop for receipts and bank statements to generate an expense audit
- SOP Doc Generator: produces standard operating procedures for workflows you demonstrate
- Google Calendar Assistant: books meetings by reasoning over your actual availability
- Price Comparison Shopper: checks multiple sources without manual site-by-site navigation
- Subscription Cleaner: mass unsubscribes from newsletters and spam in bulk
- Deep Research Assistant: generates comprehensive research reports on any topic
- Investment Due Diligence: Claude acts as personal financial assistant using your own documents
