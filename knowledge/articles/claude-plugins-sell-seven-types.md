Following a prior post that got 300K+ views, Corey Ganim lays out seven specific Claude plugins targeting service businesses (agencies, consultants, freelancers), each with a defined buyer, pricing range, and rationale for why it sells. The plugins are: client onboarding automation, weekly report generator, content repurposing engine, client renewal/upsell intelligence, proposal/SOW generator, customer support knowledge base, and lead research/outreach. Pricing runs $500–5,000 setup plus $200–500/month maintenance. The business model works because most small service businesses have these workflows but lack technical staff to automate them.

Part 2 covers monetizing Anthropic's official plugins (which are free) through three approaches: done-for-you configuration services ($2K–5K), niche workshops ($200–500/person, then sold as recorded course), and bundling official plugins with custom skills to sell as vertical-specific "kits." The configuration service has the highest margin since the underlying software is free and the value is expertise.

The article closes with simple math: one custom plugin sold to 10 clients at $2K setup + $300/month = $56K year-one. The window is framed as open because most business owners know AI exists but can't implement it themselves — the implementation gap is the product.

## Key Takeaways

- Seven high-value plugin categories for service businesses, all addressable without writing code — the skill is identifying the workflow gap and configuring Claude correctly
- Client onboarding, proposal generation, and weekly reporting are the highest-leverage targets because they consume hours of billable time on repetitive, low-creativity tasks
- Official Anthropic plugins create a monetization arbitrage: free tools + paid configuration expertise = high-margin productized service
- Niche workshops (e.g., "Claude for Real Estate Agents") have higher perceived value than generic AI training and can be recorded and sold as courses for passive revenue
- The implementation gap between AI awareness and AI deployment is the core business opportunity; plugin sellers are essentially the channel between Anthropic and small business adoption
