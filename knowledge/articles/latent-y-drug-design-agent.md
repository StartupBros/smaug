Latent Labs launches Latent-Y, described as the world's first autonomous agent for drug design that has been lab-validated end to end. Takes a research goal and autonomously reasons, designs, iterates, and delivers lab-ready antibodies with biological reasoning at the level of a PhD protein design expert. Can operate fully autonomously or collaboratively.

The launch includes a technical report, blog post, and access application. The system bridges AI-driven design with actual laboratory validation — closing the loop between in silico design and wet lab confirmation.

## Key Takeaways

- First autonomous drug design agent claimed to be lab-validated end-to-end (not just in silico)
- Specializes in antibody design with PhD-level protein design reasoning
- Operates autonomously from research goal to lab-ready output, or collaboratively with researchers
- Local-first / privacy-focused framing based on tweet description
- Technical report available at latentlabs.com
