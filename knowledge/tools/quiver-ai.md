QuiverAI is an AI lab and product company focused on frontier vector design. Their first model, Arrow-1.0, generates scalable vector graphics (SVGs) from images and text prompts, automating the design process for vector graphics.

The company raised an $8.3M seed round led by Andreessen Horowitz (a16z), signaling investor confidence in AI-driven vector design as an emerging application area.
