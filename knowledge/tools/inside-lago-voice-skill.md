An open-source Claude Skill template for capturing an individual's writing voice, created by Anh Tho Chuong (Head of Content at Lago). The template was reverse-engineered from years of her own hand-written posts, emails, and edits — specifically the delta between what AI drafts and what she actually sends. Rather than trying to encode taste, it captures anti-patterns, structural preferences, and formatting rules that save hours per week while preserving the soul behind the writing. Anh is a non-native English speaker who writes shorter, more direct sentences, and her skill embraces this as a feature rather than a bug. Lago has a track record of 10 front-page HN posts, suggesting the underlying writing philosophy is proven.

## Key Features

- Open-source template you fill in with your own voice
- Captures anti-patterns and the gap between AI drafts and what you actually send
- Designed for repetition, structure, and formatting — not emotion or authenticity
- Built from real post history and edits, not AI-generated rules
- Works as a Claude Skill for weekly writing workflow acceleration
